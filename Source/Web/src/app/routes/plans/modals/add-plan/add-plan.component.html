Behavioral Health Initiative (BHI) care plans are designed for behavioral health care management between one provider and one patient.
Chronic Care Mangement (CCM) care plans are for patients with two or more chornic conditions who are not considered complex patients.
Complex Chronic Care Management (CCCM) care plans are designed for patients with two or more chronic conditions who are considered complex patients.

Transitional Care Management (TCM) care plans are designed to support the patient between the transition of care from one care provider to another.
